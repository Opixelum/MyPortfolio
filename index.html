About

Then, in 2021, I started digging into blockchain development, particularly in Web3. I learned how blockchains work, Solidity and so on. That's when I landed my first tech jobs.

After that, in 2022, I realized that I could accomplish bigger projects and contribute more to the advancement of the world with AI. That's when I started to learn more about the data science, deep learning and mathematics behind this technology.
